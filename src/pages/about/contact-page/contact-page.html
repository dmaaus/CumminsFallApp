About Park Hours

Cummins Falls State Park is open from 8 AM to 6 PM.
Please remember to leave the waterfall area by 5 PM to make it to the parking area by closing time.

The Gorge closes at 5 PM

 Ongoing & Future Closings Park Location

The park address is:
390 Cummins Falls Lane
Cookeville, Tennessee 38501

Take the GPS directions to Cummins Mill Road. Drive about 1/2 mile past the point shown on the GPS and turn left on Blackburn Fork Road. Drive about 300 yards and turn left. Amenities

- Restrooms
- Water Fountains
- Free Parking
- Life Jackets (At the Gorge)
- ADA access available upon request (Overlook only)
- Designated picnic area above the falls
